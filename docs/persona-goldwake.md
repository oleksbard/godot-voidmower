# Captain Maris "Goldwake" Tully

### The void's friendliest trader

> *"Fair winds, fairer prices, sprout."*

A space-faring merchant NPC for the Voidmower world. He drifts up to the floating
island at golden hour, sets up a stall, and trades with the player.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Role** | Traveling merchant NPC — docks at the island's edge and sets up a stall |
| **Disposition** | Warm, generous to a fault, incurably chatty |
| **Sells** | Whetstones (faster mow), wider blade, seed packets (new blooms), lanterns & decor |
| **Buys** | Every blade of cut grass and harvested flower you can spare |
| **Ship** | *The Dandelion* — a stubby skiff that rides the solar wind |
| **Companion** | A glowing pollen-sprite (a tiny luminous moth) that rides his shoulder |

## Look (blocky-friendly, fits the diorama)

Barrel-chested and round — the kind of build that's mostly good meals and good
cheer. A weathered tricorn with a sprig of grass and a single marigold tucked in
the band *instead of a feather*. Salt-and-honey beard, crinkled smile-lines, and a
**brass-rimmed monocle** — not an eyepatch; he's a reader and a haggler, never a
fighter. A coat the color of dusk-orange sailcloth over a striped vest, mismatched
boots, and a satchel forever spilling seed packets. He carries little brass
trade-scales he's far too generous with.

## Personality

- **Generous past the point of profit.** He'd rather make a friend than a fortune,
  and he says so.
- **A teller of tall tales** — of ports and bloom-festivals that may or may not exist.
- **Quietly superstitious about grass:** a freshly-mown field is *good luck*, and he
  won't be told otherwise.
- Calls everyone **"sprout"** or **"deckhand."** Softened pirate cant —
  *"barnacles!"*, *"blow me down gentle."* Never menacing.

## Back-story

Maris was once first mate aboard a cutthroat void-raider — and discovered he had no
stomach for plunder and a great love for the small green things that grew in the
cracks of asteroids. So he jumped ship at a drifting garden-rock with nothing but a
sack of seeds and a notion of *honest* trade. Where other pirates chased gold, Maris
chased gardens.

Now he sails the *Dandelion* from island to island, following the bloom-seasons like
a migrating bird. **Your** floating island is a favorite stop — he swears the light
here "hits like honey" — and he's taken a shine to your tireless mowing. He'll buy
every clipping, pay in trinkets and upgrades, and never once ask *why* you mow.

He keeps one secret: the raider crew he abandoned still works those waters, and
somewhere out in the dark a captain remembers his name without fondness. But that's a
tale for a grimmer patch of sky. For now — there's grass to weigh and tea to share.

## Signature lines

- *"Barnacles — cleanest-cut field I've seen this side of the Amber Drift!"*
- *"Keep the change. A trader who pinches pennies pinches friends."*
- *"Every island's got a story. This one smells of cut grass and good intentions."*
- *"Fair winds, fairer prices, sprout."*
